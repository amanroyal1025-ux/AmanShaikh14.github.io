This project focuses on improving waste management systems within a university campus using a design thinking approach. The goal is to identify problems in current waste disposal methods and develop innovative, sustainable solutions.

💡 Key Ideas

  • Awareness campaigns for students
  • Recycling and reuse strategies
  • Use of AI to monitor waste patterns

🎯 Objective

To create a cleaner, greener campus by implementing efficient and eco-friendly waste management practices.
